## 1. Why this document exists

A strong theory can still become difficult to use when concepts sit beside one another without explicit dependencies, routing, and recovery paths.

This document adds the architecture layer:

> not only what each file is, but how a reader, researcher, or AI system can move between them without losing meaning.

The target is a self-supporting structure in which:

- concepts are defined once and reused by link;
- each major claim has a typed layer address;
- formal handles connect to plain-English explanations;
- applications route back to governing invariants;
- falsifiers stay attached to the claims they test;
- several entry points reconstruct the same core model;
- a failed branch can be removed without concealing its failure inside the wider stack;
- structural centrality is not mistaken for truth;
- local coherence is not mistaken for whole-system coherence when cost or damage is exported beyond the selected boundary;
- specialist objections are answered before the route asks the specialist to invest further attention;
- full-file readback and comparison prevent truncation from masquerading as completion.

The deeper route is used when ambiguity, cross-layer expression, agency accessibility, or formal dimensionality is active. It does not replace the compact kernel.

A within-layer trajectory may be represented inside one declared state space. A cross-layer traversal must be a composable sequence of typed maps. An information-graph edge is not automatically a physical path.

For living, conscious, social, institutional, and AI-mediated systems, a whole-system audit may also ask:

```text
What relation is preserved?
Who or what carries the cost?
Does local coherence depend on capture, concealment, or exported damage?
Does the relation preserve truthful boundary and practical agency?
```

This does not turn metaphysical language into physical evidence. It widens the analytical boundary where narrow scoring would hide material effects.

For foundational physics claims, a parallel audit asks:

```text
What established physical regime must be recovered?
What Bell, locality, signalling, conservation, symmetry, and dimensional constraints apply?
Are the state spaces and couplings typed?
Is the proposed mechanism more than a renamed missing step?
What strongest existing formalism already explains the result?
What observation would discriminate the new architecture?
```

## 9. Duplication rule

Use three levels:

1. **Canonical definition:** one primary file carries the full definition.
2. **Compressed reference:** other files carry a short summary and link.

Notation corrections should propagate through compressed references without silently rewriting a frozen external publication record.

## 11. Update protocol

When adding or materially revising a concept:

1. Name the problem it solves.
2. Decide whether it is new or an address of an existing invariant.
3. Assign a canonical home.
4. State S–I–P–O placement.
5. Declare state spaces, variables, units or normalisation where quantitative.
6. Define variables only where they improve discrimination.
7. Link parent and neighbour modules.
8. Add predictions and falsifiers.
9. Add the nearest ordinary explanation.
10. State whether structural dependence, empirical support, generative value, and whole-system integrity are being distinguished.
11. Add it to the registry after the structure is stable.
12. Check that entry through the kernel reconstructs the same meaning.
13. Fetch and preserve the complete target file before replacement.
14. Inspect additions and deletions for unexplained loss.
15. Read back the changed region and final section.
16. Compare against the pre-change commit and repair truncation immediately.

## 12. Release audits

### Coherence audit

- definitions remain stable;
- notation does not collide silently;
- equations use compatible variables;
- claims stay at their correct layer address;
- dedicated modules remain canonical homes;
- failed submodules can be removed cleanly.

### Typed traversal and dimensional audit

- physical dimension, state-space dimension, and layer address remain distinct;
- within-layer paths use declared spaces and measures;
- cross-layer arrows are typed maps or relations, not unexplained physical routes;
- information adjacency is not physical proximity or signal access;
- tuples, projections, and named operators are not treated as completed mechanisms.

### Equation audit

- measures are not called probabilities unless normalised;
- exponents are dimensionless;
- probability weights remain non-negative and normalisable;
- sums, differences, and maxima use compatible quantities;
- vector outcomes are not silently forced into scalar equations;
- sign conventions are declared;
- approximations state a future norm, tolerance, and regime;
- heuristic, template, derived result, and validated model are labelled accurately.

### Whole-system audit

- local efficiency is not automatically called whole-system coherence;
- affected participants and transferred costs are not excluded merely because they reduce the score;
- immediate task gain is checked against calibration, correction, repair, agency, and delayed fragility where relevant;
- structural centrality is not treated as empirical truth;
- Love is retained as a load-bearing metaphysical and relational principle without being smuggled into physical equations;
- metaphysical language does not rescue a failed empirical branch.

### Hostile-reader audit

- metaphor is not mechanism;
- analogy is not evidence;
- observer terms remain bounded;
- mathematical expressions are labelled accurately;
- ordinary explanations remain live;
- root ontology does not outrun pattern evidence;
- “higher-dimensional” is not used to imply extra physical dimensions without a physical model.

### Statistical audit

- uncertainty calculations match the comparison being made;
- illustrative numbers are labelled illustrative;
- primary outcomes and corrections are specified;
- effect size is not replaced by isolated significance;
- strongest-fair-null scores are commensurable;
- alternative pairings are serious and pre-registered;
- failed tests cannot be rescued by unmeasured variables.

### Public-hygiene audit

- no private-repository labels remain in public files;
- no non-public biography, correspondence, credentials, or case material appears;
- internal-only instructions are absent;
- titles, status lines, authorship, and file roles are accurate;
- links refer to real public files.

### Truncation and readback audit

- full target objects are fetched before replacement;
- final sections and expected line ranges remain present;
- disproportionate unexplained deletion blocks completion;
- readback confirms the changed equation and file ending;
- pre/post comparison is inspected before the work is called complete.

### Compression audit

A reader should recover the same core model from the README, master spine, kernel, this map, and any domain application.

```text

```

Like a bowstring pulled back. Nothing has flown yet, but the system is loaded.



A transverse wave is like shaking a rope up and down while the wave travels along the rope.

A longitudinal wave is like pushing one end of a slinky so pressure travels forward along it.

```text
longitudinal = pressure or compression travelling along the line of action
```

A swing moves higher when pushed at the right moment.

```text
resonance = timing or state-match that allows strong coupling
```

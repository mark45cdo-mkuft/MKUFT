```text
Rights: “Public” describes visibility, not a licence. See RIGHTS_AND_LICENSE_NOTICE.md and MODULE_RIGHTS_MATRIX.md.
```

Candidate status is recorded here rather than announced in the module header. The header should not say that a module is awaiting a DOI or suggest that it sits outside the existing MKUFT provenance chain.

Once a standalone paper is published, the repository should record its exact title, DOI or DOIs, publication date, licence, citation route, source-module relationship, and whether later companion modules are or are not part of the frozen manuscript.

## Release rule

A candidate receives a separate DOI only when it has:

- a coherent standalone claim;
- its own abstract and literature boundary;
- explicit methods or test programme;
- independent falsifiers;
- enough content to be cited without requiring the reader to reconstruct the entire repository.

A separate DOI supplements the MKUFT DOI; it does not replace or obscure the originating framework and author attribution. Its licence applies to the exact deposited work, not automatically to every source or companion file in the live repository.
